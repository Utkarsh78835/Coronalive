Coronavirus Symptoms

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash Your Hands Regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand sanitizer

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or fixed elbow when you cough or sneeze

Avoid close contact (2 meter or 6 feet) with people who are unwell

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell

Stay informed try watching news & follow the recommended practises

If you have fever , cough and difficulty breathing. seek medical care immediately
